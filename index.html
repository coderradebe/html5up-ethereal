Education

All people have a right to education. Technology is able to provide greater access, even in places where resources are too scarce to allow this. Artificial intelligence, machine learning in particular, will be used to create individualized learning, ensuring no child is left behind.

Democracy

The strength of democratic institutions will be upheld by technology in the future. Decentralized platforms will help electoral commissions, especially in the developing world, to reduce voter fraud and rigging. This video shows how blockchain technology will change the world. Use number 10 pertains to voting systems.

Access to Information

The ability to use the internet should be seen as a fundamental human right in today's world. It has been shown that internet access leads to higher rates of education and health, which are in themselves fundamental in the fight against poverty.
